David Caldwell, MD, PhD

San Francisco, CA

I am a neurosurgery resident at UCSF, who completed my MD and PhD at the University of Washington through the MSTP program in 2021. Prior to this, I obtained my BSE and MSE in biomedical engineering at the University of Michigan.

My PhD was in bioengineering in the laboratories of Dr. Jeffrey Ojemann in the department of Neurosurgery and Dr. Rajesh Rao from the department of Computer Science and Engineering. My PhD research work focused on engineering direct electrical stimulation of human sensorimotor cortex.
